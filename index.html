About

With over fifteen years of experience in the industry Kita Mountain utilizes the latest technology stacks to provide custom software solutions that meet clients' needs.

Specializing in API integrations leveraging cloud architectures, Kita Mountain implements automated processes to increase efficiencies for clients' business operations including chatbot automation implementations.

Kita Mountain frequently analyzes existing client processes using legacy applications in order to revamp and develop modern designs converting on-prem monolithic application architectures into robust cloud-based implementations.

From client-facing applications, to internal tools, to automated testing in CI/CD pipelines, Kita Mountain can provide the necessary solutions to any client organization.
